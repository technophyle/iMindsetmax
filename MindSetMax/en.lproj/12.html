1.2 Mindset

Having a mindset means to act from a set of core beliefs and values, that enhance your state of well being regardless of what is going on in the external world of business, personal relationship hassle or what the broadcast media choose to show you that day.

A mindset is a set of assumptions, methods, or notations held by one or more people or groups of people that is so established that it creates a powerful incentive within these people or groups to continue to adopt or accept prior behaviors, choices, or tools.
Changing your mindset is the beginning to changing your life.

It's quite characteristic that there are several kinds of mindsets (= the thoughts that exist in our heads), and that the people who succeed have a special mindset that few know about. We think approx. 12,000 - 50,000 thoughts every day (National Science Foundation, USA) regardless of intelligence, so I'm sure you can imagine that a positive, supportive foundation of thoughts will have a great impact on your day and future life !
