What is META-Health?

META-Health is the art, science and practice of Body-Mind-Social Health with a focus on how specific stress triggers, emotions and beliefs affect specific organ symptoms. META-Health is based on Integrative Medicine and Prevention and is practiced by all types of health professionals interested in a precise and effective Body-Mind-Social diagnosis and therapy. META-Health Professionals are doctors, naturopaths, psychologists, nurses, therapists, counselors and health care providers.
